Inter link Glass and Alluminum Works is a leading company in Alluminum and Glazing works and the scope of our services can be divided into three parts: Consulting, Quality assurance & Control and Technical services.

Services

Consulting Services

Interlink carries out all work in strict accordance with, in-house project control procedures which encompass all aspects of project management including:

  • Planning
  • Scheduling
  • Man-hour management
  • Cost monitoring control
  • Technical performance
  • Productivity
  • Control of changes and installation.
Quality Assurance & Control

Quality Assurance and Control System has been prepared in accordance with the International Organization for Standardization Quality Systems Model ISO 9001, and is maintained to ensure that all work is executed to the highest standards of technical excellence and is delivered on time and within the budget.

Interlink definition of Project Quality Assurance is the ability to control the production process by planned and systematic sequences of actions which provides the confidence that all works carried out will satisfy the project requirements for Project management, Technical content and Fabrication ability.

Technical Services

We have extensive experience of a vast majority of technical skills and services which includes without limitation:

  • Curtain walling / Facades
  • Conventional Curtain wall system
  • 2 & 4 sides Structural Glazing
  • Aluminium Doors and Windows
  • Aluminium / Stainless Steel handrails and balustrades
  • Metal cladding / Composite Panels
  • Fire rated Steel Glazed Doors, Partitions & Curtain wall
  • Aluminium fire rated Glazed Doors, Partitions & Curtain wall.
  • Security / Decorative clusters
  • Frameless Facades / Spider Systems
